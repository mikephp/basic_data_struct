Adding new application
The easiest way to add new application is just Drag'n'Drop from your Desktop or Explorer.
But here is another way. You may create profile for application.
Press the button "New profile" on the toolbar, or from menu "File->New application". You should see the window like the picture below.
Here you can edit by your hands or let the FreeCap fill by pressing the "Browse" button.
FreeCap will scan program for all necessary information and put it into profile:.
